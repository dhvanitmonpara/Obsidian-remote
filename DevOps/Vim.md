## **Modes**

- `i` – insert
    
- `v` – visual
    
- `V` – visual line
    
- `<Esc>` – back to normal
    

---

## **Movement**

- `h j k l` – left/down/up/right
    
- `w` / `b` – next/prev word
    
- `0` / `$` – start/end of line
    
- `gg` / `G` – top/bottom
    
- `Ctrl + u` / `Ctrl + d` – half-page up/down
    

---

## **Insert / Edit**

- `i` – insert before
    
- `a` – after
    
- `o` – new line below
    
- `x` – delete char
    
- `r` – replace char
    
- `.` – repeat last action
    

---

## **Delete / Change**

- `dd` – delete line
    
- `dw` – delete word
    
- `D` – delete to end of line
    
- `cw` – change word
    
- `cc` – change whole line
    

---

## **Copy / Paste**

- `yy` – yank line
    
- `yw` – yank word
    
- `p` – paste
    
- `P` – paste before
    

---

## **Search**

- `/text` – search
    
- `n` / `N` – next/prev result
    
- `*` – search word under cursor
    

---

## **Visual Mode**

- `v` – select
    
- `d` / `y` – delete / yank
    
- `>` / `<` – indent / unindent
    

---

## **Undo / Redo**

- `u` – undo
    
- `Ctrl + r` – redo
    

---

## **Save & Quit**

- `:w` – save
    
- `:q` – quit
    
- `:wq` / `ZZ` – save & quit
    
- `:q!` – quit no-save
    

---

## **Indent & Formatting**

- `>>` / `<<` – indent / unindent
    
- `==` – auto-indent line
    

---

## **Jumps**

- `%` – jump to matching bracket
    
- `Ctrl + o` / `Ctrl + i` – jump back/forward
